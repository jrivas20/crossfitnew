PARTNERSHIP WITH THE ORLANDO HEALTH FOUNDATION

We believe that Crossfit and Fitness are activities to be enjoyed by everyone!. That's why our partnership with Orlando Health is so important for us. Our Adaptive Program at SOCF Crossfit with the help of Orlando Health Foundation provides people with disabilities and/or special needs the opportunity to experience a variety of fitness workouts. We offer exciting opportunities for all levels, from beginners to experts.
